Research Interests

  • Teleoperation and shared autonomy

  • Human-robot interaction
  • Soft robotics and wearable robotic systems
  • System integration for real-world deployment

About

Staff Engineer at the Digital Twin Center, Samsung Electronics (Sep. 2025–present). Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Seoul National University (Aug. 2025), advised by Prof. Yong-Lae Park in the Soft Robotics and Bionics Lab. B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology (Dec. 2015, High Honor).

Research spans fiber-optic sensing, robot hands, teleoperation, compliant actuation, and full-stack robotic system integration.
